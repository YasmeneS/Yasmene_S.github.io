French Toast

Fluffy french toast with fruit

Servings: 2 people

Prep time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

Four slices of bread

3 Eggs

2 tsp Vanilla

1 tbsp Sugar

2 tbsp butter

1 tbsp Cinnamon

1/2 cup of milk

Toppings:

Whipped cream (optional)

Powdered sugar (optional)

Fruit of your choice

Directions

1. Heat up a pan on the stove on medium heat.

2. Get a medium bowl and crack eggs, put in milk, cinnamon, sugar and vanilla.

3. Whisk together ingredients until fully mixed

4. Use this time to melt the butter in the pan and get 4 slices of bread

5. Soak both sides of each slice of bread in the mixture and put in pan until both sides are lightly toasted and golden brown

6. Stack two slices on top of each other and add the syrup, fruit of your choice, powdered sugar(optional),and whipped cream(optional).

7. Serve and enjoy


Toasted Sandwich

Hot toasty sandwich

Servings: 1-2 people

Prep time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

4 slices of bread

Cheese of your choice

2 tbsp Butter

Spinach

Ham or turkey slices

1 tbsp Garlic powder

1 tbsp Parmesan cheese

Cilantro

1 tspLime juice

1 tsp salt

1 Avocado

1 tsp olive oil

Directions

1. Heat up pan on medium heat.

2. Grab avocado and slice in half emptying the inside in a small bowl or cup

3. Mash up avocado and mix in garlic powder, salt, cilantro and lime juice.

4. Get slices of bread and put turkey or ham on it, cheese of your choice, spread avocado on top of the meat and cheese,and put spinach on top, then the other slice of bread on top

5. Repeat steps for second sandwich

6. Spread butter on top of bread and put sandwhich in pan one at a time until both sides are lightly brown and crisp

7. Serve and enjoy


Pizza

Cheese pizza

Servings: 6 people

Prep time

Ingredients

2 1/2 cups warm water

Mozzarella and ricotta cheese

Tomato sauce

Olive oil

2 tsp active dry yeast

7 cups all-purpose flour

1 tsp sugar

1 1/2 tsp salt

Basil

Dried oregano

Directions

1. Make the yeast rise by sprinkling the sugar and yeast in the warm water. Let sit for 10 minutes, until bubbles form on the surface.

2.In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the middle and add the olive oil and bloomed yeast mixture. Using a spoon, mix until a shaggy dough begins to form.

3. Once the flour is mostly hydrated, turn the dough out onto a clean work surface and knead for 10-15 minutes. The dough should be soft and smooth. Form the dough into a circle.

4. Grease a clean, large bowl with olive oil and place the dough inside, turning to coat with the oil. Cover with plastic wrap. Let rise for at least an hour, or up to 24 hours.

5. Punch down the dough and turn it out onto a lightly floured work surface. Knead for another minute or so, then cut into 4 equal portions and shape into rounds.

6. Lightly flour the dough, then cover with a kitchen towel and let rest for another 30 minutes to an hour while you prepare the sauce and any other ingredients.

7. Preheat oven to 450-500 degrees fahrenheit. Put baking sheet or skillit in oven.

8. Stretch and press the dough into a thin round. Make it thinner than you think it should be, as it will slightly shrink and puff up during baking.

9. Sprinkle flour onto an upside down baking sheet and place the stretched crust onto it. Add the sauce and ingredients of your choice.

10. Slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or pan. Bake for 15 minutes, or until the crust and cheese are golden brown.

11. Serve and enjoy

Delicious chocolate s'mores milkshake

Servings: 1-2 people

Prep time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

Marshmallows

Milk chocolate

2 graham crackers

Chocolate or vanilla ice cream

2 tbsp Sugar

Chocolate syrup (optional)

2 cups milk

Ice

Whipped cream (optioal)

Appliance:

Blender

Directions

1. Take the graham crackers and chocolate and crush them up into tiny pieces.

2. Pour milk, sugar, about 2 ice cubes, 2 to 3 scoops of ice cream, graham crackers, chocolate, and chocolate syrup in blender and blend it.

3. When done pour in cup and top it off with some whipped cream (optional), chocolate syrup, chocolate and graham cracker pieces, and some marshmallows.

4. Serve and enjoy
